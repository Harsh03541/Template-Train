Trains have a rich history, dating back to the early 19th century. The first steam-powered locomotive, built by George Stephenson, was introduced in the early 1800s, revolutionizing transportation and industry. The Indian Railways network spans across the length and breadth of the country, covering almost every part of India. It connects remote villages to major cities and serves as a lifeline for millions of people.Indian Railways has been undergoing modernization and technological advancements in recent years. Projects like the introduction of high-speed trains (e.g., Vande Bharat Express), the implementation of Wi-Fi services at stations, and the development of dedicated freight corridors are aimed at enhancing efficiency and passenger experience.
